Sweet song, easy to play. You will need some leslie or tremolo effect
for the intro. But a quick and soft vibrato will work as well.
I transcribe one guitar (not always the same !) but 
it work quite well. For the arpeggios I don't transcribe the real
notes, just play it on the chord.
