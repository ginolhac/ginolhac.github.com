Album: Some Boots

My favourite from Some Boots! Such a beautiful song. This was a little 
tricky to figure out for me but now I think everything should be right. 
It's fairly simple to play except for one chord where you have to stretch 
your hand a little, but I'm sure you can manage it!

Two things: 1. Geoff sometimes plays and arpeggiates the chords a little 
differently in the two verses. I won't bother writing it out, as long as you
know the chord you'll be alright, just listen to the song!    

2. I didn't type the solo since it's a lot of feedback and very long. The solo
is actually basically played over a slightly modified blues progression. The key
is G minor so use the G Dorian, blues scale or minor scale and improvise. If you
are good at controlling feedback and such this would obviously sound cool here!
Good luck!
