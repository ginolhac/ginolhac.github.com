Author/Artist: Geoff Farina
Title: The Same Way
Album: Usonian Dream Sequence
Transcribed by: Philip Tanimura
Email: funkymonk@rocketmail.com

Just a beauriful song from Usonian Dream Sequence. It really conveys that 
feeling of emptiness and loss after a breakup. 

It's very simple to play really. I left out the electric guitar overdubs
because I'm lazy and I figured most people will play this solo anyways. 
They should be easy to pick out if you want to, they're all in the C-major 
scale. Except for that it's mostly standard open chords.
