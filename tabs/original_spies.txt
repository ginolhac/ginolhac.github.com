I have to admit I'm a little proud of figuring this one out. The chords were
a bit of a struggle. At first I thought Geoff was playing the same chords in 
many places but as I listened I found he changes them around while keeping one 
note constant. This song is pretty interesting chordwise especially the part 
after the second solo where there's this chord cycle that sounds like the verse 
but is actually different chords. I have no idea why these substititions work
harmonically though. Oh well...

I just tabbed the beginning of the solo phrase. The rest I'll leave up to you
to improvise - Key of C-minor. The X:es represent muted strokes. I only wrote 
them out in the beginning but they continue throughout the song.
