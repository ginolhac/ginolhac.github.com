Album: Karate
Transcribed by: Philip Tanimura
Email: funkymonk@rocketmail.com

The great ending song from the first album, many peoples 
favourite from that record I guess. This song has two 
guitar-parts sometimes, so when it does I tabbed it as 
Guitar 1 and Guitar 2. Guitar 2 is mostly little fills 
here and there so it works fine on one guitar (in that 
case stick with Guitar 1). 

I really enjoy playing this song and it was one of the 
highlights for me when I saw Karate live the last time. 
Hope you like it too!
