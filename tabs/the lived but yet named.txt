To be honest this is my least favourite song from Unsolved.
But it's a lot more fun to play than to listen to I think. 

I had a very hard time figuring out the chords for the Interlude 
part but I hope it's correct now (if you have other suggestions 
you can mail me). It's a quite tricky song with very jazzy chord 
changes. I didn't tab the solo but the key is A-minor or you can
play the changes if you want. The solo is over the verse chords 
I think but I'm not 100% sure.
